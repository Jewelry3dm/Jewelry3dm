I’m a freelance 3D jewelry designer, with +4 years of experience

Let me introduce myself. I am from Lviv (Ukraine), my name is Valery, I am 24 years old. I have been engaging in jewelry modeling for more than 4 years. I worked as a 3D jewelry designer in two Ukrainian jewelry companies at different times. I have been working in UpWork since 2018. Jewelry modeling interested me even while I was studying at Lviv National University. Currently, I successfully did more than 400 individual orders. Additionally, in the process of work, I consulting with my father, who has been manufacturing individual jewelry for more than 24 years and worked for 18 years as a Main Technologist in a one Lviv jewelry company. I operate with software such as Matrix, Rhino, T-splines, Keyshot. At this time I'm also studying Zbrush. Besides, I have little experience in casting, casting processing, stone fixing, and polishing. I gave it more than 6 months for a better understanding of the manufacture of 3D models. So, I have a complete understanding of the process of producing jewelry from 3D CAD to finished products. I hope that my skills and experience will be useful to you.
